Core Technology

The "6S+EDR" Innovative Safety System

Built on 20+ years of power generation expertise, integrating IoT, AI, and edge computing with advanced energy storage — pioneering the world's first energy storage EDR system.

BWS
Battery Warning System
Data-driven algorithm with intelligent advance warning — detects gradual failures 28 days in advance, preventing battery cluster fires before they occur.
EDR
Event Data Recorder
World's first for energy storage (patented). Multi-source data collection with comprehensive recording for credible accident tracing and root cause analysis.
HCS
High-speed Control System
Real-time Ethernet technology achieving high-speed synchronization and low-delay regulation — reducing frequency regulation latency to under 50ms.
EMS
Energy Management System
Dynamic intelligent control with system-level partition safety management. On-premises deployment available at three scale tiers with full data security.
OMS
Operations & Maintenance
Real-time monitoring with automatic work order generation and visible progress tracking — maximizing uptime and maintenance efficiency.
BMS
Battery Management System
Connector temperature detection with PACK-level fire prevention linkage — multi-layer protection at the cell, module, and system levels.
PCS
Power Conversion System
Series multi-branch design providing effective electrical and safety isolation — ensuring fault containment and system reliability.
