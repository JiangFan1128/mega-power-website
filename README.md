\# MEGA Corporate Website



\## Project Overview

Build a premium corporate marketing website for \*\*MEGA POWER CO., LTD.\*\* based on the provided company PDF and the uploaded HTML style reference.



MEGA should be presented as an \*\*integrated digital green energy solutions provider\*\*, not merely a hardware vendor. The site should communicate MEGA's capabilities across:

\- utility-scale energy storage

\- frequency regulation storage

\- integrated PV-ESS-charging

\- transportable energy storage

\- commercial \& industrial energy storage

\- digital platform, safety, and O\&M services



The company PDF positions MEGA around integrated green energy, combining photovoltaic generation, storage, charging, and digital management tools. It also organizes its business across the solution families above. :contentReference\[oaicite:1]{index=1} :contentReference\[oaicite:2]{index=2}



\## Primary Goal

Create a modern, multilingual website that:

1\. explains what MEGA does clearly

2\. leads with scenarios and customer needs

3\. highlights Japan as a key market

4\. builds trust through platform, safety, and service capabilities

5\. prepares the structure for future expansion



\## Target Audience

\- B2B customers

\- EPCs / project developers

\- grid and renewable project stakeholders

\- EV charging infrastructure investors

\- industrial and commercial energy users

\- Japan-based partners and clients



\## Language Strategy

This website must support:

\- English (`/en`) as the source language

\- Japanese (`/ja`) as the key localization language

\- Chinese (`/zh`) as an additional localization language



All pages should share the same structure across locales.



\## Positioning Statement

MEGA delivers integrated digital green energy solutions for grid, industry, transport, and charging scenarios, combining core equipment, digital management platforms, and service capabilities.



\## Key Narrative

The site should be \*\*scenario-first\*\*, not product-catalog-first.



Visitors should quickly understand:

1\. what scenarios MEGA serves

2\. what problems MEGA solves

3\. how the solution system works

4\. why MEGA is safe, reliable, and suitable for Japan

5\. what products support each solution



\## Japan Market Emphasis

Japan should be visible as a strategic market, not only in company identity but also in content priorities.



The content should reflect needs such as:

\- limited site conditions

\- high reliability expectations

\- resilient power deployment

\- safe and maintainable systems

\- operational stability

\- local support readiness



\## Content Sources

\- Company source PDF: use it as the main business and product source

\- Uploaded HTML reference: use it as a design and layout direction reference only
